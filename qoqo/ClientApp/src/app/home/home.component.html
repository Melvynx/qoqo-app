How it's work?

When you click on the button the counter increments by 1, then 10 seconds later you have the opportunity to reincrement the button by 1.

To be the winner you will have to click the button at the right time.

Example:

The objective is to have a 54

  • The button is currently at 52
  • You click on the button, it changes to 53 and you have to wait 10 seconds
  • 10 seconds later you click and it changes to 54 so you just won!

But be careful, all users are connected and can all click at the right time. Let's say you clicked on the button at 53 and another user clicks on the button at 54, he will win...
